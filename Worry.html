The W. O. R. R. Y. Protocol - Make a plan

What? – Answers the question of what exactly you want to achieve. This is a clear and attainable goal, because without it, there is no direction for action. When? – Answers the question of when you will take action. A time frame helps you avoid procrastination and gives your plan structure.

How? – Answers the question of how you will implement your plan. These are specific actions you will take to reach your goal. Adapt – Answers the question of how you will adapt if you face obstacles. Flexibility helps you stay on track and find new ways to achieve your goal. Why? – Answers the question of why it’s important for you to do this. A clear reason helps you stay motivated and persevere, even when it's hard.
